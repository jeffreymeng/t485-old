Welcome to Scouting!

Join scouting today with Troop 485! Scouting is a fun experience for boys 11 to 18 years old. We do all kinds of outdoor activities such as camping, swimming, hiking, and rafting. Come to one of our troop meetings to join!

Troop Meetings

We meet the 1st and 3rd Monday of each month at the Home of Christ 5 Church from 7:30pm to about 9:30pm.
