Council Resources

The Silicon Valley Monterey Bay Council makes a direct and positive impact to over 23,000 youth in our community through a variety of fun and challenging experiences. Through commitment to our program, youth acquire valuable life skills and a sense of community, while building lifelong friendships!

Silicon Valley Monterey Bay Council is divided into 7 districts, Coyote Creek, Loma Prieta, Pioneer, Polaris, Quicksilver, San Benito, and Santa Lucia. Each district provides leadership from the district executive, district chairman, and district commissioner. Districts are directly responsible for the operation of Scouting units (packs, troops and crews) by providing resources for Scouts and volunteers.

For more information about SVMBC, check out Council Resources page.

District Resources

Each district has three main volunteers that are a tremendous resource for troop; District Commissioner, District Chair and District Executive.

The District Commissioner’s main role is to help us succeed. They also oversee the unit recharter renewal plan so that each unit registers on time.

The District Chair helps committee chairs recruit an adequate number of members to carry out the functions of the district, plan and preside district committee meetings, and help attain district goals.

The District Executive’s main role is to help each unit in the district. He have a variety of flyers and forms available for units, can help answer questions or get us in contact with the correct volunteer, and ensure we have a successful Scouting year.

T485 is under Polaris District which serves the area of Saratoga, Cupertino, and Sunnyvale.

District Roundtable

Each district has a monthly roundtable meeting for all troop leaders – Scoutmasters, assistant Scoutmasters, and troop committee members. Each roundtable meeting provides quality resources, knowledge and skills to help unit leaders deliver an outstanding program to their Scout packs or troops. Polaris District Roundtable is located at our church on 7:30 PM, second Tuesday of each month.
